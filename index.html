About Us

Joining the Hubbard Heights Men's Club allows you to take part in events that could earn you Pro Shop money simply for playing your normal weekend round with another club member. Live scoring on weekly and special events is kept on the Unknown Golf app, which is included in your membership fee. The app also keeps live, up to date money list earnings and player of the year points.

We have weekly club events from April through October and several team events against other clubs in the region. To qualify for our weekly events, simply sign up at tee off time on your app (Thursday, Friday, Saturday or Sunday), play the round with another club member, and input your score as you play.

Join today by visiting the Pro Shop at E. Gaynor Brennan Golf Course or clicking the button below.
